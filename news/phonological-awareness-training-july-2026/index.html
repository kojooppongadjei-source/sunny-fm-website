Phonological Awareness Training — July 17, 2026 at Sunny FM

Phonological Awareness is a child's ability to hear, identify, and work with the sounds in spoken words — and it is the #1 predictor of reading success. This one-day powerful training will equip teachers, school leaders and parents with the tools to build strong readers from the very beginning.
Led by Anita S. Whyte, EdS. — Literacy Coach, Educator, Trainer and Reading, Phonics & Writing Specialist — this is a rare opportunity to learn directly from one of the most experienced literacy coaches visiting Ghana from Atlanta, Georgia.
