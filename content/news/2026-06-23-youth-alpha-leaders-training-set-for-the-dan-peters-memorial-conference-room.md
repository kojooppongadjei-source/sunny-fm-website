The training is designed to equip youth leaders with the knowledge, skills, and practical tools needed to effectively run Alpha courses among young people. Participants will gain valuable insights into creating welcoming environments where young people can explore life's big questions, discuss faith openly, and encounter Jesus in a meaningful way.

Alpha has become one of the world's most widely used evangelistic tools, helping millions of people across different cultures and denominations engage in conversations about faith. The Youth Alpha model specifically focuses on reaching teenagers and young adults through relatable content, authentic discussions, and community-building experiences.

Speaking about the importance of the training, organizers say there is a growing need for churches and ministries to intentionally engage the younger generation with the gospel in ways that are relevant and accessible. The Youth Alpha Leaders Training aims to empower participants to become effective facilitators who can guide young people on their faith journey while fostering genuine relationships and discipleship.

The event also presents an opportunity for church leaders, youth pastors, campus fellowship executives, and ministry volunteers to network with like-minded individuals who share a passion for reaching the next generation for Christ.

The training forms part of Alpha Ghana's ongoing efforts to strengthen evangelism and discipleship initiatives across the country, while Sunny 88.7 FM continues its commitment to supporting programs that promote spiritual growth and Christian leadership development.

For more information, interested participants may contact **+233 54 500 8250** or **+233 55 804 5513**.

The training is free
