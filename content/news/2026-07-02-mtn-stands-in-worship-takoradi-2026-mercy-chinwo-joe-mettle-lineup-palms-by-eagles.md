The **MTN Stands in Worship** gospel show returns to Takoradi this week, with the annual thanksgiving and praise event set for **Friday, July 3, 2026** at the **Palms by Eagles Auditorium**.

Now one of the most anticipated dates on Ghana's gospel calendar, MTN Stands in Worship Takoradi brings together top local and international gospel acts for a night of praise and worship — and this year's lineup is no exception.

### Full Lineup: Who's Performing at MTN Stands in Worship Takoradi 2026

This year's stellar lineup includes:

* **Mercy Chinwo** (International Guest)
* **Joe Mettle**
* **Ps. Joe Beecham**
* **Amy Newman**
* **Steve O & The Broken Chains**

Nigerian gospel star Mercy Chinwo headlines as the event's international guest, joining Ghanaian favourite Joe Mettle — fresh off his own **[Praise Reloaded 2026](/news/joe-mettle-invites-christians-to-gather-to-lift-jesus-at-praise-reloaded-2026/)** concert — alongside Ps. Joe Beecham, Amy Newman, and Steve O & The Broken Chains.

### Event Details: Date, Time and Venue

* **Date:** Friday, July 3, 2026
* **Time:** Gates open at 5:00 PM; the programme begins at 6:00 PM
* **Venue:** Palms by Eagles Auditorium, Takoradi
* **Dress code:** White, with a touch of yellow

### How to Win Free Tickets to MTN Stands in Worship

Organisers **[MTN Ghana](https://www.facebook.com/MTNGhana/)** are running ticket giveaways on their official social media pages ahead of the event. To stand a chance of winning, fans are encouraged to comment on MTN Ghana's posts with their favourite song from any of the performing artists.

**Sunny TV** will be bringing live updates from MTN Stands in Worship Takoradi via our social media channels — follow along for highlights from the night as Mercy Chinwo, Joe Mettle and the rest of the lineup lead Takoradi in a night of thanksgiving and praise.
