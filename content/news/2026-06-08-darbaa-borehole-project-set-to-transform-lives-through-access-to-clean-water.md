Speaking on *Positive Soul Food* on Sunny 88.7 FM, Dr. Francis Osei, National Coordinator of e3 Partners Ministry, highlighted the importance of the initiative and the impact it is expected to have on the community. e3 Partners Ministry is collaborating with Sunny FM to bring the project to fruition.

According to Dr. Osei, access to safe and clean drinking water remains one of the most pressing needs in many underserved communities. He explained that the Darbaa Borehole Project is aimed at providing a sustainable source of clean water, helping to improve health outcomes, reduce the burden of travelling long distances in search of water, and enhance the overall well-being of residents.

Dr. Osei emphasized that providing clean water is more than a development project—it is an expression of practical Christian love and a powerful tool for community transformation. He encouraged individuals, churches, businesses, and organizations to support initiatives that bring hope and tangible solutions to vulnerable communities.

The discussion also underscored the importance of partnerships in driving meaningful change. Dr. Osei noted that lasting transformation becomes possible when people unite around a shared vision to meet the needs of others and build stronger communities.

Listeners were inspired by the conversation, which highlighted the power of compassion, service, and collective action. The Darbaa Borehole Project serves as a reminder that a simple intervention can have a life-changing impact for generations to come.
