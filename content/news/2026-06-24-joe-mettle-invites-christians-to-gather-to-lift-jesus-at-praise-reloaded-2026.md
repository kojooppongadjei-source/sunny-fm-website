summary: Renowned gospel minister Joe Mettle has extended a heartfelt invitation
  to Christians across Ghana and beyond to attend Praise Reloaded 2026,
  scheduled for Sunday, June 28, at the Accra Sports Stadium.
---
Speaking during an interview on **Sunny 88.7 FM** with Jennifer J Danquah on the Inspirational morning drive, Joe Mettle shared his excitement ahead of the annual worship gathering and expressed his desire to see believers unite in one accord to exalt the name of Jesus.

"My prayer is that the Christian community will come together and worship as we lift the name of the Lord on high," he said. "The Bible says that if He is lifted up, He will draw all men unto Himself. We believe God will move powerfully as we gather to worship Him."

Praise Reloaded has become one of Ghana's most anticipated worship events, drawing thousands of worshippers for an atmosphere of prayer, praise, and life-changing encounters with God. This year's event is expected to feature powerful worship moments and an opportunity for believers to come together in unity and faith.

Joe Mettle also used the opportunity to express his appreciation to partners who have supported the vision over the years. He specifically thanked **Sunny 88.7 FM**, **Sunny Gold Brown Sugar**, **Ecobank** and a host of other partners too numerours to mention for their unwavering support.

Importantly, the award-winning worship leader emphasized that **Praise Reloaded 2026 is completely free to attend**. However, participants are required to register in advance nonetheless he was emphatic that your inability to register should not stop your from coming to the stadium on Sunday.

As preparation toward June 28, Joe mettle and his team will be having praise and worship sessions online starting this evening.
