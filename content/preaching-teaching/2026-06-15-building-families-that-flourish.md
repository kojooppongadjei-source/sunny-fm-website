In this teaching, Elder Amos Kevin-Annan walks through the F.L.O.U.R.I.S.H principles — a practical framework for building strong, faith-centered families.

Topics covered include:

- Faith as the foundation of family life
- Love that reflects God's love
- Open communication between spouses and children
- Unity in purpose and direction
- Respect for one another
- Investing time intentionally
- Service to one another and the community
- Hope that anchors the family through challenges
