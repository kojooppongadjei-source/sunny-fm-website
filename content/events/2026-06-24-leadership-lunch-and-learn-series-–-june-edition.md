  Professionals, entrepreneurs, executives, and aspiring leaders are set to

  The event, organized by Social Influence, will take place on Saturday, June 27, 2026, at 1:00 PM at Ghastro Restaurant, Cantonments, Accra.


  The session will feature two distinguished speakers who have made significant contributions in the areas of leadership, communication, and personal branding.


  Dr. Glory Edozien, an Executive Visibility Advisor and Curator of the Top 100 Career Women in Africa, will share insights on how professionals can strategically position themselves for influence, impact, and career growth.


  Joining her is Joyce Daniels, an Executive Communication Coach and Global Speaker, who will offer practical guidance on leveraging effective communication to build authority, expand opportunities, and increase professional visibility.


  The event is convened by Abena N. Chrappah, founder of Social Influence, whose work focuses on helping individuals and organizations amplify their impact through strategic leadership and visibility.


  According to the organizers, "The Audacity of Visibility" is designed to challenge participants to step beyond their comfort zones, embrace their unique value, and intentionally position themselves for greater influence in their industries and communities.
